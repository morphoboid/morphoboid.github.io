Self-Supervised Deep Metric Learning for ancient papyrus fragments retrieval

This web page is related to the paper "Self-Supervised Deep Metric Learning for ancient papyrus fragments retrieval"
The dataset proposed in the paper can be downloaded here here. It is based on the University of Michigan Papyrology collection
This dataset is tailored for page retrieval, it is composed of 4579 fragments that can be reconstituted into 1118 papyri, with an average number of fragments per papyri of 4.4, and a standard deviation of 6.4.
